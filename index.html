How It Works

  1. Measure the dimensions of your canvas (Length × Width × Depth).
  2. Select your preferred frame thickness and style.
  3. Receive a detailed quote and confirm your order.
  4. Deliver your canvas to us, or arrange a collection within Secunda / Trichardt for a small fee. Alternatively, securely package your canvas and send it via PostNet (please note: fragile item).
  5. We also offer a DIY option: we can prepare the frame without your canvas and provide screws and instructions for assembly. Please note that returns are not accepted if the frame does not fit your canvas correctly.
  6. Once your frame is ready, we will carefully return it to you.

Additionally, you can provide us with a digital image. We can print it on canvas of your chosen size, construct the frame, and send the finished piece directly to you.
