How It Works

  1. Measure your canvas (L × W × H).
  2. Choose your frame thickness.
  3. Receive a quote and accept.
  4. Deliver frame to us / we do collections in Secunda / Trichardt area for extra fee / bubblewrap your canvas and send it via PostNet (fragile item).
  5. Optionally, we can build the frame for you without your canvas and send you screws + instructions (no returns if frame does not fit).
  6. We make the frame and send it back to you.

Or, send us your photo and we can print it on the canvas size of your choice, build the frame, and send it to you.
